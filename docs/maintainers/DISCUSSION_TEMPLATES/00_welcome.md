# Welcome to schemachange Discussions

Welcome to the community! This is the place to ask questions, share ideas, and connect with other users.

## How to Use Discussions

**Q&A** - Got a question? Ask here! Search first to see if it's been answered.

**Ideas** - Have a feature idea? Discuss it here before creating an issue.

**General** - General conversation about schemachange, workflows, and best practices.

**Show and Tell** - Share what you've built with schemachange.

## Found a Bug?

Please [create an issue](https://github.com/Snowflake-Labs/schemachange/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) instead of posting in Discussions.

## Want to Contribute?

Check out our [Contributing Guide](../CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started!

## Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive
- Search before posting
- Include details when asking for help
- Share your knowledge by answering questions
